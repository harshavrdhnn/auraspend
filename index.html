Mobile / Device Sharing Guide

To access this tracker on your mobile device or other tablets in your network:

  1. Expose Python server on PC: python -m http.server 8080 --bind 0.0.0.0
  2. Find your local IP address in command prompt: ipconfig (e.g. 192.168.1.5)
  3. Open the browser on your phone and navigate to: http://<your-ip>:8080
